Revenue Analysis Dashboard
Overview

The Revenue Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze organizational revenue performance across multiple dimensions, including products, employees, product categories, and states. The dashboard provides insights into both top-performing and bottom-performing entities, helping stakeholders identify growth opportunities and areas requiring attention.

The report includes year-based filtering, allowing users to compare performance trends across different periods.

Features
1. Year-wise Analysis
Interactive year slicer (2018–2022)
Dynamic filtering across all visuals
Easy comparison of revenue performance over time
2. Top Performers

Displays the Top 5:

Products by Revenue
Employees by Revenue
Product Categories by Revenue
States by Revenue
3. Bottom Performers

Displays the Bottom 5:

Products by Revenue
Employees by Revenue
Product Categories by Revenue
States by Revenue
4. Interactive Filtering
Cross-filtering between visuals
Dynamic updates based on selected year
Drill-down analysis capabilities

🛠️ Tools & Technologies

Interactive Visualizations
